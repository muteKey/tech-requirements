The system will offer opportunities to connect, collaborate, and ultimately transform the health of consumers, athletes, military personnel, and clinical rehab markets, while the extensive content expands beyond strength training into lifestyle and educational programs covering nutrition, health, mindfulness, and overall wellness topics.
For athletes and consumers, assessment of velocity, power, and force is important since varying these measures can yield significant results. Currently, this type of comprehensive testing is conducted in laboratories or special facilities which can be cost-prohibitive, difficult to access, and limiting. With the OxeFit system, assessment, coaching, training and measurement occurs simultaneously and work synergistically with existing technology. Real-time feedback on these metrics will help them achieve the most efficient workout while post-workout analytics will generate the next workout for optimal effectiveness.
